Looking to learn more?

Coding Catchup is the home for all of my blog posts that I have written along my journey to my first web development job and beyond. I started writing on DEV, however, I also wanted to have my own home on the web outside of my portfolio site so here we are!

I started writing after a friend encouraged me to do so, ensuring me that it will make me more valuable to future employers. Shortly after I started, I fell in love with sharing my journey and my followers on DEV blew up. However, burnout eventually occurred and I had a short absence. This new blog platform is my re-emergence into the blogging world.

Through all of the ups and downs on this journey, I've continued to want to write and share. I hope you find value in these posts, following my journey, as well as any technical posts I write.

Thank you for reading!
